Instructions:

  1. Enter your name and choose a category you want to be quizzed on.
  2. Click on the “Start Game” button to begin the quiz.
  3. You will be presented with a question and four answer options.
  4. Select the answer you believe is correct by clicking on it.

  6. The game will continue until you have answered all the questions in the category.
  7. At the end of the quiz, you will see your final score and other stats.
  8. Have a look at the Leader Board to see how you’re doing.
  9. Try to get the best accuracy score and become the quiz master!
